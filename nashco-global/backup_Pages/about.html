Our Story

Founded in 1994 in Dubai, Nashco Global has expanded to a multinational group with operations in industrial plastic manufacturing (injection moulding), automotive sales/imports/exports, spare parts distribution, HFC refrigerant distribution, and general trading across UAE, Oman, KSA, and beyond.
